BOOM BOOM SHIP

Hello there and welcome to BOOM BOOM ship, a game where your goal is to sink all the ships that are present on the board.
Depending on the difficulty you select, there will be a different amount of ships at varying lengths. You are able to sink the ships by clicking
on a box inside the board, and it will tell you if you missed or hit. Once you are ready press "Play Game" and enjoy the game!
